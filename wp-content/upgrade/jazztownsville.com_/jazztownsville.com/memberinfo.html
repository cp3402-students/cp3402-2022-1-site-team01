In 2020, the Club presents a wide variety of jazz styles at two different venues.

Chico Rio (upstairs) 247 Flinders Street Friday 7 to10 pm and The Fish Inn on The Strand  Sunday 5 to 8 pm.


Both venues are air conditioned and have meals available.  
Cover charge for regular sessions: members $10, non-members $15. Cover charge may be higher on special nights.

Refer to Program page for current information regarding each venue.

Membership runs from January to December each year.
